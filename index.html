Ink3D

Sculpting 3D Assets with Extremely Complex Textures via Video Generative Models

Goal

Aims to Paint Complex 3D Textures (text, fine patterns, etc.)

TL;DR

Ink3D combines the rich appearance priors of video generative models with the geometry strength of native 3D representations, enabling complex textures with coherent boundaries:

  1. Generate orbital videos, not sparse multi-view images.
  2. Back-project dense observations into o-voxels.
  3. Repair texture artifacts in native 3D space.
